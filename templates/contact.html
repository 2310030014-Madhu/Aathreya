Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Supporting Our Mission

How are donations used?

We maintain complete transparency in our operations. 85% of donations go directly to educational programs, 10% to administrative costs, and 5% to fundraising efforts. We provide detailed reports on how every dollar is spent.

Can I visit the programs?

We welcome supporters to see our programs in action. Contact us to arrange a visit to one of our partner schools or community centers. Seeing the impact firsthand is incredibly rewarding.

Do you accept international donations?

Yes, we accept donations from supporters worldwide. We work with international banking partners to make the donation process simple and secure for contributors from any country.

How can businesses get involved?

We offer various corporate partnership opportunities, including sponsorship programs, employee volunteer initiatives, and matching gift programs. Contact us to discuss custom partnership options.
